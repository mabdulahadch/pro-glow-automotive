

# Protomotive.pk — Premium Car Detailing Landing Page

## Brand Setup
- Dark theme with navy-black background (#0a0d14), cyan-blue gradient accents (#2b8fd4 → #5bc4f5), white text
- Google Fonts: Orbitron (headings), Inter (body)
- Upload the Protomotive logo as a site asset

## Page Sections (Single-Page Layout)

### 1. Hero Section
- Fullscreen dark background with animated particle/gradient effect in cyan/blue
- Centered logo, tagline "Where Precision Meets Passion"
- Two CTAs: "Book Appointment" (cyan filled) + "View Our Work" (outlined)

### 2. Stats Strip
- 3 animated counters: 1,055+ Jobs Done / Happy Clients / Years in Business
- Scroll-triggered count-up animation

### 3. Services Grid
- 6 service cards (PPF, Vinyl Wrapping, Ceramic Coating, Oil Change, Auto Detailing, Complete Restoration)
- Hover tilt effect with cyan glow border

### 4. Before/After Gallery
- Interactive drag-to-reveal image comparison slider
- "Our Work Speaks" heading, 4+ comparison pairs with placeholder images

### 5. Why Choose Us
- 4 icon feature boxes: Expert Team / Premium Products / DHA Location / 10AM–9PM Daily

### 6. Testimonials Carousel
- Auto-scrolling review cards with star ratings
- Google branding badge, real review content from brief

### 7. Gallery / Instagram Feed
- Masonry grid of car work photos (placeholders)
- Hover overlay with cyan gradient + Instagram icon linking to @protomotive.pk

### 8. Book Appointment Form
- Dark styled form: Name, Phone, Car Model, Service dropdown, Date picker
- "Schedule My Visit" cyan CTA button (front-end only, no backend)

### 9. Location & Contact
- Embedded Google Map (Plot 413 DHA Phase 8, Lahore)
- Contact card with clickable phone (0300-5005666), hours table

### 10. Footer
- Logo, quick links, social icons (Instagram, WhatsApp), copyright, cyan top border

## Navigation & UX
- Sticky glassmorphism navbar with backdrop blur
- Smooth scroll to sections
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Floating WhatsApp chat button (bottom-right, all screens)
- Floating pulsing "Book Now" button
- Click-to-call on mobile
